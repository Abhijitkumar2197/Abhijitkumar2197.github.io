About

I got into Salesforce the practical way — by being handed real problems on live orgs and figuring out how to solve them properly. Two years in, I've led a regional rollout for a European client, built a real-time notification system for a behavioral-health platform, and refactored more legacy Apex than I'd like to admit.

What I care about most is ownership: if I ship it, I monitor it, tune it, and keep it healthy in production. That means security done right — OAuth, field-level security, sharing models — and code that respects governor limits instead of fighting them.

Right now I'm spending my spare cycles on the AI side of the platform: predictive data enrichment, smart automation triggers, and AI-assisted decision flows with Agentforce.
